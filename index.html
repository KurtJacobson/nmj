General Info • History

Since 1983, I have provided construction consulting services, including commercial and residential building condition surveys, pre-purchase home inspections, maintenance and repair consulting and planning, and litigation support and expert testimony in construction disputes. I work primarily in the Atlanta GA metro area, but travel routinely to other parts of Georgia and surrounding states. I have a BS in Civil Engineering (Univ. of NM, 1979).
